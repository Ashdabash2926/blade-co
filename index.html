Our Philosophy

About
Blade & Co.

"The chair doesn't just change how you look — it changes how you feel. Every cut is a craft. Every client deserves a moment that's entirely theirs."

— James Whitfield, Founder

Opened in 2014 on the edge of the Northern Quarter, Blade & Co. was built on a simple idea: that a great barbershop should feel like a refuge. Somewhere the conversation is as sharp as the scissors and the standards are always uncompromising.

Today we serve hundreds of clients every month from across Leeds — men who care about how they present themselves and trust us to keep them looking their best.

The People Behind the Blades

Meet the Team

James Whitfield
Master Barber & Founder

With over 15 years in the trade, James founded Blade & Co. after apprenticing in Savile Row and honing his skills in Manhattan's finest shops. His speciality is skin fades and precision scissor work that few can match.

Marcus Reid
Senior Barber

Marcus brings an artist's eye to every cut. Having trained in Amsterdam and worked the competition circuit, he's the man for intricate designs, textured crops and keeping your beard sharper than a straight razor.

Danny Okafor
Barber

Fresh talent with an old-school attitude. Danny trained through the Blade & Co. apprenticeship programme and has quickly become a client favourite — known for his relaxed chair-side manner and obsessive attention to necklines.
